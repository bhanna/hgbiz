Galia Aharoni

Galia Aharoni is licensed to practice law in California and Louisiana.  She has been practicing law since 2009.

Galia has started two small businesses, served as a Business Development Attorney for a large publishing company, and practiced law with both private firms and government organizations.

She attended Tulane Law School on a Dean’s scholarship where she received Honors in Trial Advocacy, served as Senior Articles Editor for the Journal of Law & Sexuality, published an article on intellectual property law, and held leadership roles in a variety of clinics and organizations.

Galia is particularly known for her writing skills, big-picture strategic thinking, diplomatic yet direct communication style, and her ability to put people at ease.  When not working (and sometimes while working), she can be found hanging out with her dog, drinking too much coffee, reading, or collecting new hobbies like welding, glass blowing, and beekeeping.

Contact Galia at galia@hgbusinesslaw.com.

Heather Satin

Heather Satin was first licensed to practice law in Canada in 1998, and more recently in California.

Heather thrives on challenge and feels at home on the road less traveled.  Throughout her notable career, she has helped shape and lead organizations large and small, from solos to substantial corporate enterprises.  She is known statewide for her success working with many of California’s top legal publications and software applications, including awesome organizations like The Rutter Group, the Witkin Legal Institute, Thomson Reuters, and Continuing Education of the Bar.

Like all entrepreneurs, Heather knows what it’s like to wear many hats when starting a business.  She enjoys being a creative-thinking business strategist, entrepreneurial mentor, complex-contract negotiator, software project manager, prosecutor, public defender, author, and educator.  But above all, Heather is a straight-talking, trustworthy, and dedicated advisor.

As a business owner, legal partner, (half-)marathoner, lover of languages (guess which ones she speaks), and parent of two preteens, Heather understands the demands of launching and growing a healthy business while maintaining excellent work-life balance...and your sanity!

Contact Heather at heather@hgbusinesslaw.com
